HogwartsRegistry

Sorting Hatsare for babies!

Enrolling Hogwarts students is what this AngularJS app is all about.


Let's be honest here, times are changing, and and registration time for Hogwarts is becoming a bit of a hassle. I mean, it's gone totally bollocks! Our owls are getting a wee bit old, and forget about preparing all those acceptance letters!


Scabbers always nibbles up my good paper, and those potty chocolate frogs are always making a mess of my office!

In light of this, I began working on a student registration redesign. One that needn't be funded by Gringotts, but by my own perseverance ...and Bertie Bott's jellybeans. Needless to say, some of the Angular features displayed are filtering, services, routing, and directives. The best part is that enrolling students is quick and easy, like finding all of Voldemort's horcruxes in an Easter egg hunt (Jk!)


And now to all of my Witch, Wizard, Muggle, and Death Eater friends out there: I entrust in you, the task of making the world a more efficient place through the use of these smashing web technologies, and I'll see you on the other side of the Forbidden Forest.
